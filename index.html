Introduction

Dogs are human's friends. This is especially true in megacities such as NYC, where people are busy surviving and need accompany after their work. The trend of having a dog is pervasive in NYC, but how to carefully breed our dogs is not clear to each dog owner.

This website will serve as a guidebook for each potential and already dog owner, providing information about dog runs, pet supply stores, dog-friendly hotels, vet clinics, dog licenses, dog shelters & breeders, and dog friends service.

Overall, the web application will provide resources as much as possible for dog raisings in this way to make sure each dog can have healthy life and give good accompany to their owners.
